
# Apna Fashion Mart - Detailed Marketplace Project Documentation

# 1. Project Overview

Apna Fashion Mart is not a normal ecommerce website. It is a premium location-based multi-vendor fashion marketplace platform designed to connect local clothing shops with nearby customers through technology-driven commerce and discovery systems.

The platform combines the concepts of:
- ecommerce marketplaces,
- local fashion discovery,
- hyperlocal commerce,
- vendor management,
- customer engagement,
- and marketplace administration.

The goal is to create a scalable fashion-tech startup ecosystem where users can discover nearby clothing stores, browse their products, place online orders, or physically visit stores based on their location.

This platform is intended to evolve into:
- a web application,
- Android app,
- iOS app,
- and a full marketplace technology platform.

---

# 2. Core Business Idea

The business model focuses on helping local fashion retailers gain digital visibility while helping customers discover nearby clothing stores more efficiently.

Instead of functioning like a normal single-brand ecommerce website, Apna Fashion Mart operates as a marketplace where:
- multiple shop owners join the platform,
- customers discover shops using location,
- users browse products from nearby stores,
- and the platform earns through commissions and marketplace activity.

The marketplace acts as a bridge between:
- local fashion businesses,
- and digital consumers.

---

# 3. Marketplace Ecosystem

The entire platform is based on a three-role ecosystem:

1. Customer/User
2. Shop Owner/Vendor
3. Admin/Platform Owner

Each role has separate access, permissions, dashboards, and functionalities.

# 7. Core Marketplace Functionality

## Hyperlocal Discovery

The platform’s primary feature is location-based fashion discovery.

Users should:
- allow location access,
- or enter a location manually,
- and discover nearby fashion stores.

### Radius-Based Search

- 5 km
- 10 km
- custom ranges

### Shop Discovery

- nearby clothing stores,
